Ichigo Kurosaki

About Ichigo Kurosaki

Ichigo is a substitute soul reaper from an animated show called bleach live's to protect his family and friends in the world of the living and on the soul society.
While on this path he makes some new friends and powerful new allies but also some formidable foes as well.
With determination, the help of his friends and sheer will Ichigo is able to gain the strength he needs to protect the ones he loves,
in process unknowingly becomes the most powerful soul reaper of all.
