About Orihime Inoue

Orihime Inoue is a fictional Human living in Karakura Town. She is a former student of Karakura High School. She is married to Ichigo Kurosaki and has a son named Kazui Kurosaki. She is one of protagonist's in the bleach series.

Orihime


Orihime is a friendly quiet girl as a child her and her younger brother were raised by their mother and father,
however it was not the best childhood. Their father was a drunk and their mother was a prostitute who would argue
and beat their children. On his 18th birthday in fear that their parents would kill Orihime, who at the time was only 3 years old
he took her and ran away from their parents raising her on his own. Years later he would sadly pass on due to a fatal car accident
leaving Orihime essentially all alone. It was around this time that she would meet Substitute Soul Reaper Ichigo Kurosaki
who would go on to save her life from the soul of her deceased brother who had become a Hollow.
However neither of them could know that this was only the start of something that would change their lives forever.
